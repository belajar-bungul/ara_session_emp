Employee-Based Session Auth – Login, Track, and Force Logout by Employee

Auth

What is the Module Function?


This module lets you manage login sessions based on employees, not just users. Every login is tracked and linked to the specific employee selected—giving you full visibility into who logged in, when, and from which device or browser. Need to force logout an employee who forgot to sign out on a public device? No problem. Suspicious activity from an account? You can terminate that session instantly. Perfect for companies that take account security seriously, especially those with shared devices, shift-based teams, or remote workers. With this module, you gain tighter control, better visibility, and peace of mind. Take charge of your system’s access security—employee by employee.
